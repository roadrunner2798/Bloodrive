Are You Eligible To Donate Blood?

Take this 2 min quiz to find out..

ARE YOU ELIGIBLE?

1. Are you 17 years or older?

2. Have you had a tattoo or piercing in the last three months?

3. Have you travelled outside of Canada, the continental USA, Antarctica or Europe in the last 3 weeks?

4. Have you been to the dentist for an extraction, surgery or root canal in the last three days?

5. Are you pregnant, have been pregnant or had a baby in the last six months?

6. Are you taking prescription medication(s)?

7. Do you weigh more than 50 kg (110 lb.)?

Great! You Are Eligible To Donate..
